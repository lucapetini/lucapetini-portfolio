## Overview

This project involved the development of a personal legal website for an Italian lawyer specializing in family law and citizenship, with a strong focus on international clients. The objective was to create a professional, trustworthy, and bilingual online presence capable of clearly communicating legal services across different audiences.

I handled the full end-to-end build, covering UX structure, visual design, WordPress development, and technical configuration.

## Key Features

- **End-to-End WordPress Build**: Complete project execution from UX and visual design to final WordPress implementation.
- **Bilingual Setup**: Italian and English language configuration using WPML to support international clients.
- **Elementor-Based Development**: Flexible and maintainable layouts built with Elementor.
- **Client Contact & Lead Tools**: Integration of contact forms and WhatsApp for direct communication.
- **Compliance & Security**: GDPR and cookie compliance implementation, along with basic security hardening.
- **Hosting & Performance**: Hosting and domain setup, performance optimization, and deployment.

## Technologies Used

- **WordPress Development**
- **Elementor**
- **WPML**
- **WordPress Website Design**
- **WhatsApp**

## Challenges and Learnings

Designing a legal website for a bilingual and international audience required careful attention to content clarity, tone, and navigation. Balancing professional credibility with accessibility reinforced the importance of UX structure and multilingual configuration in service-based websites.

## Outcome

The final result is a professional, bilingual, and easy-to-manage legal website that effectively supports both Italian and foreign clients. The project highlights strong capabilities in full WordPress builds, multilingual setups, and UX-driven design for professional services.
