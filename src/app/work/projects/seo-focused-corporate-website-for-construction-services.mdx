## Overview

This project involved the development of a corporate website for a construction services business with a strong focus on local SEO and lead generation. The goal was to create a clean, fast, and easy-to-manage platform capable of attracting qualified traffic from search engines.

I handled the full end-to-end build, covering UX structure, visual layout, WordPress development, and SEO configuration.

## Key Features

- **End-to-End WordPress Build**: Complete development using WordPress and Elementor, from structure to final deployment.
- **SEO-Oriented Setup**: Optimized URLs, page titles, XML sitemap, and robots configuration to support search engine visibility.
- **Service-Focused Pages**: Clear and structured service pages designed to convert organic traffic into leads.
- **Lead Generation Tools**: Contact and quotation request forms to support inbound inquiries.
- **Direct Contact Widgets**: Floating WhatsApp and email widgets to enable quick customer interaction.
- **Performance & Manageability**: Clean structure optimized for speed and ease of client management.

## Technologies Used

- **WordPress Development**
- **Elementor**
- **SEO Performance**
- **Website Security**
- **UX & UI Design**

## Challenges and Learnings

A key challenge was balancing SEO requirements with user experience. Structuring content to satisfy both search engines and real users reinforced the importance of clear information hierarchy, performance optimization, and conversion-oriented design in SEO-driven projects.

## Outcome

The final result is a fast, SEO-ready corporate website designed to generate qualified leads through organic search. The project highlights strong expertise in WordPress development, SEO-focused setup, and conversion-oriented UX design.
