## Overview

This project involved the development of an institutional website for a national organization focused on promoting Renewable Energy Communities (CER). The platform targets citizens, associations, and public administrations, with the objective of making community-based energy solutions accessible and understandable to non-technical users.

I handled the full end-to-end build, including UX structure, visual design, and WordPress development using Elementor Pro.

## Key Features

- **End-to-End Institutional Website Build**: Complete development from UX planning to final WordPress implementation.
- **UX for Non-Technical Users**: Clear information architecture and layouts designed to simplify complex energy topics.
- **Elementor Pro Development**: Modular and scalable sections built to support future content and service expansion.
- **Integrated Forms**: Contact, information request, and service registration forms integrated into the platform.
- **Scalable Structure**: Modular content blocks designed for long-term growth and adaptability.

## Technologies Used

- **WordPress Development**
- **Elementor**
- **UX & UI Design**
- **Corporate Website**
- **API Integration**

## Challenges and Learnings

A major challenge was translating a complex regulatory and technical subject into a clear and approachable digital experience. This project reinforced the importance of UX-driven design, modular structures, and clarity when building institutional platforms aimed at diverse audiences.

## Outcome

The final result is a clear, accessible, and scalable institutional website that supports the promotion and adoption of Renewable Energy Communities. The project demonstrates strong expertise in full WordPress builds, UX design for public-facing platforms, and long-term structural planning.
