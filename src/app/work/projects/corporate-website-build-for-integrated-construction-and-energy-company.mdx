## Overview

This project involved the development of a corporate website for an Italian company offering integrated construction, maintenance, and energy-related services. The target audience included public administrations, companies, and SMEs, requiring a clear and authoritative institutional presence.

The main objective was to position the company as a single technical partner capable of managing the full process from design to execution. I handled the entire build, including UX structure, visual direction, and WordPress development.

## Key Features

- **End-to-End Corporate Website Build**: Complete development from UX planning to final WordPress implementation.
- **Institutional UX & UI Design**: Clear and professional layout designed to communicate reliability and technical expertise.
- **SEO-Ready Structure**: Optimized content hierarchy and page structure to support organic visibility.
- **Lead & Quotation Forms**: Contact and quotation request forms to support business inquiries.
- **External Platform Integration**: Integration with a third-party platform for Renewable Energy Community (CER) management.
