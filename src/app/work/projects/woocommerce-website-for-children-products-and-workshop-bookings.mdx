## Overview

This project involved the development of an eCommerce website for a business offering high-quality children products alongside in-person educational workshops. The platform was designed to serve families and parents, combining traditional product sales with an integrated online booking system.

I handled the full end-to-end build, from UX and visual design to WordPress and WooCommerce development, ensuring clarity, usability, and long-term scalability.

## Key Features

- **WooCommerce E-Commerce Setup**: Complete WooCommerce configuration for product sales, including variable products and inventory management.
- **Workshop Booking System**: Integration of a booking management system with online payment support for in-person workshops.
- **Payment & Shipping Configuration**: Setup of payment gateways and shipping rules tailored to the business model.
- **Parent-Friendly UX**: Clear navigation and layouts designed to simplify purchasing and booking for families.
- **Performance-Oriented Build**: Optimized structure focused on speed, reliability, and scalability.
- **End-to-End Delivery**: Full project execution from concept to deployment.

## Technologies Used

- **WooCommerce**
- **WordPress**
- **Ecommerce**
- **Booking Management System**
- **Payment Gateway Integration**

## Challenges and Learnings

The main challenge was balancing two different flows—product purchasing and workshop booking—within a single, coherent user experience. This project reinforced the importance of UX clarity, structured checkout flows, and performance optimization in hybrid eCommerce platforms.

## Outcome

The final result is a clear, scalable, and easy-to-manage WooCommerce platform that successfully combines product sales with workshop bookings. The project demonstrates strong expertise in WooCommerce development, booking system integration, and user-focused eCommerce design.
